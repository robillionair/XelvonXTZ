Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

At Xelvon XPT (hosted on robillionair.com), we respect your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, store, and protect your information when using this interface.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum required data to provide access to the interface:

  • Email Address: Captured strictly to authenticate access and manage system limits.
  • System Signatures: Basic request parameters (IP, User Agent, Timestamp) are logged when checking requests to prevent abuse and spam.

2. How We Store Your Data

Emails are saved securely in Cloudflare KV storage. We utilize zero third-party analytical integrations, cookie tracking systems, or commercial advertising scripts.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your email identifier is strictly used to unlock terminal access. We do not sell, rent, share, or market your personal information to third parties.

4. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your email signature at any time. Simply reach out to the site administrator to have your record removed from our KV databases.
